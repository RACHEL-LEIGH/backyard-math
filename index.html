How it works

Enter your project measurements, choose your material depth or product size, and get a quick planning estimate. These tools use standard area and volume formulas, then add optional overage where homeowners commonly need extra material.

For the most reliable estimate, use product dimensions and material weights from your local supplier whenever available.
